Contact & Help

Having trouble using Copilot, or encountered a possible bug? Asking questions or reporting bugs can both be done through the issue tracker at GitHub. This helps us keep track of open bugs and problems, and allows us to easily comment and discuss the issue.

As Copilot consists of several packages, each of them comes with its own issue tracker. If you have a question about a specific package or encountered a problem specific to a package, please report the problem at that package, if possible. In all other cases, please refer to the issue tracker of the general Copilot package:

  • copilot (General issues)
  • copilot-core
  • copilot-language
  • copilot-libraries (Issues with any of the functions defined in the library)
  • copilot-c99 (Issues with generating C code)
  • copilot-theorem

General Questions

For general questions about Copilot, questions that are unsuited to be posted publicly or if you are not in the possession of a GitHub account, please send your questions to Frank Dedden dev@dedden.net.

Copilot developers
