Contact & Help

Having trouble using Copilot, or encountered a possible bug? Asking questions or reporting bugs can both be done through the issue tracker at GitHub. This helps us keep track of open bugs and problems, and allows us to easily comment and discuss the issue.

General Questions

For general questions about Copilot, questions that are unsuited to be posted publicly or if you are not in the possession of a GitHub account, please send your questions to the maintainer, Ivan Perez ivan.perezdominguez@nasa.gov.
